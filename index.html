Recent affected area

Feni district in Bangladesh is prone to frequent flooding due to its low-lying geography and proximity to rivers. During the monsoon season, heavy rainfall often leads to river overflows, inundating large areas of the district. Floods in Feni disrupt agriculture, damage homes, and displace thousands of residents, creating significant challenges for the local population.
